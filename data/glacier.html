Wildfires in Glacier National Park

Over the course of my life, I have been to Glacier National Park numerous times and consider it one of my favorite places in the country. In recent years in particular, the Western United States have been ravaged by wildfires during the drier seasons and Glacier National Park has seen their fair share. As part of one of my first GIS courses, I needed to create my own map about something that interested me. As a result, I spent a good amount of time researching Glacier National Park and exploring what vector data was available. One thing there was no shortage of was wildfire data, so I chose to compare the 20th and 21st centuries in terms of wildfire prevalence and severity. It was the first time I had created any map using DEM and a transparency to create a 3D effect. This map was also my first experience playing with masks to highlight the focal point of the map and push the rest into the background.
