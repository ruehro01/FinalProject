Have wildfires been increasing in recent years?

Fire has always been a natural and integral part of a healthy ecosystem. Periodic fires that were often started by lightning were a way for a rebirth of the ecosystem. Some plants even rely on gentle fires as a part of their life cycle, including lodgepole pine, eucalyptus, and banksia. These plants have cones or fruits that are sealed with resin and require gentle fire to open them and release their seeds.
